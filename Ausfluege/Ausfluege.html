Museum - Kultur

Visit the Museum and learn about the history of the city. The museum is located in the city center and is easily accessible by public transport.

Radtour - Sport

Make a trip to the countryside and enjoy the beautiful nature. You can rent a bike at the hotel and we will provide you with a map of the area.

Schiffahrt - Erlebnis

Take a boat trip on the river and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The boat leaves from the city center and takes you on a tour of the city and its surroundings.

Wanderung - Gesundheit

Take a boat trip on the river and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The boat leaves from the city center and takes you on a tour of the city and its surroundings.

Volleyball - Sport

Play a game of volleyball with your friends on the beach. The beach is located 10 minutes from the hotel and is easily accessible by public transport.

Saunieren - Entspannung

Relax in our sauna and enjoy the beautiful view of the city. The sauna is located on the top floor of the hotel and is open from 8am to 10pm.

Mountainbiken - Sport

Go mountain biking in the mountains and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The mountains are located 30 minutes from the hotel and are easily accessible by public transport.

Fallschirmspringen - Action

Experience the thrill of skydiving and enjoy the beautiful view of the city from above. The skydiving center is located 1 hour from the hotel and is easily accessible by car.

Fußball - Zusatz

Play football on our ervergreen field surrounded by nature!

Schwimmen - Zusatz

Swim in the lake near the hotel or go ice bathing in the ever-cold river.

Tennis - Zusatz

Play tennis when you want to with other hotel visitors or with locals from the city!
